[Ontology Name]: Description back to ToC

Flight satisfaction ontology The ontology represents and organizes knowledge related to satisfaction surveys of flights, focusing specifically on passengers' opinions about Qatar Airways and Ryanair.

Find_Survey_By_Airline&Dateback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Rule to identify a survey of an airline based on a date.

Airline c(?A) , Survey c(?S) , hasSurvey op(?A,?S) , Date c(?D) , isOnDate op(?D,?S) -> Survey c(?S) , Airline c(?A)

NegativeType_Survey_By_Ratingback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Rule to classify a survey as negative based on whether the passenger has indicated a rating below 5

Survey_classification c(?X) , Rating c(?R) , lessThan(?) -> Negative c(?X)

PositiveType_Survey_By_Ratingback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Rule to classify a survey as positive based on whether the passenger has indicated a rating of 5 or higher.

Survey_classification c(?X) , Rating c(?R) , greaterThanOrEqual(?) -> Positive c(?X)
